From a Village Dreamer to a Tech Innovator.

I am Hlulani Jabulani Mathebula, an application developer and technology enthusiast born and raised in Ngove Village, a small village outside Giyani in Limpopo Province. My early education took place at Khanyisa Education Centre, where I matriculated in 2019.

While in high school, I excelled as a soccer player, competing in school and home tournaments. Beyond sports, I was an avid gamer, spending much of my free time immersed in TV games, sharpening my problem-solving skills and strategic thinking.

My passion for technology began with a deep curiosity about how gadgets worked. I would disassemble and reassemble electronic devices just to understand their inner workings, with a special interest in motherboards and system maps. This fascination paved the way for my journey into the tech world.

Today, I am a qualified Application Developer and currently pursuing my Honours in ICT at Cape Peninsula University of Technology. My journey has been filled with challenges, lessons, and remarkable experiences that continue to shape my career.
